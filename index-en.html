Iry is a hair removal salon in Kumamoto dedicated to men. We explain the real reasoning — hair cycles, skin type — clearly, and build a treatment plan around your hair volume, budget, and lifestyle.

From First Visit to Treatment

  1. Book

    Book via official LINE, Square, or phone. Let us know your preferred date and the area you're interested in.

  2. Free Consultation

    We check your hair condition and give you an estimated number of sessions and cost. No obligation to sign up on the spot.

  3. Free Patch Test

    We confirm how your skin reacts and how much discomfort you feel before starting the actual treatment.

  4. Treatment

    Treatment takes place in a fully private room. Intensity is adjusted every session to match your skin condition that day.

    We explain cooling, moisturizing, and home care, plus the best timing for your next visit.
